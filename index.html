Welcome to Challenge #3!

With Mapbox's GL JS JavaScript library, you can create interactive maps for your web applications. By looking at the source code, you can get a sense of how to make an interactive map that can tell a story. Scroll down to read more...

Section 1

Here's where you can add a narrative to describe your story about the World Cup. Where was your favorite game? What stadiums stood out this cup? Let's start with Iceland, a country of about 350,000 people that proved to be a force in the World Cup and brought us the Thunderclap.

Section 2

As you scroll through these sections, your map will fly them to the locations you've specified. A sense of place is something that a lot of people resonate with. Give your story context with a view of landscape or vary the pitch and rotation to get a new perspective.
